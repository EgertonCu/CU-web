Evangelistic Teams

These are regional teams with a main objective is evangelism by preaching Christ in different areas of the country and promote cohesion among the union members in the different regional teams

Central Evangelistic Team (CET)

CET Comprises of Members from Nyandarua, Nyeri, Kirinyanga, Muranga, Kiambu and Nairobi Counties.

More Info Contact

Nyanza Evangelistic Team (NET)

Nyanza Evangelistic Team (NET) Comprises of Members from Siaya, Kisumu, Homa Bay, Nyamira, Kisii and Migori counties.

More Info Contact

Rift Valley Evangelistic Team

RIVET Comprises of Members from Turkana, West Pokot, Samburu, Baringo, Laikipia, Narok, Nakuru, Kajiado, Trans Nzoia, Nandi, Bomet, Kericho, Uasin Gishu, and Elgeiyo Marakwet Counties

More Info Contact

Western Outreach (WESO)

Western Outreach (WESO) Is an Evangelistic Team that comprises of Members from Bungoma, Busia, Kakamega and Vihiga counties. It covers the western region of Kenya

More Info Contact

Mid-Eastern United Brethren Evangelistic Team (MUBET)

Mid-Eastern United Brethren Evangelistic Team (MUBET) Comprises of Members from Isiolo, Meru and Tharaka Nithi counties.

More Info Contact

Uttermost Evangelistic Team

UET Comprises of Members from Marsabit, Mandera, Wajir, Garissa,Machakos, Taita Taveta, Makueni, Kitui, Tana River, Mombasa, Lamu, Kwale, Kilifi and Malindi counties.

More Info Contact

Emmulatable Students and Associates Evangelistic Team (EMUSETA)

EMUSETA Comprises of Members from majorly Embu county and associated members.
